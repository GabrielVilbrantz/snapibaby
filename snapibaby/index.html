Frequently Asked Questions about Newborn Photography at Home 🤔

Do the AI newborn photos look real?

Yes. Our AI preserves your baby's real facial features — eyes, nose, and skin tone — and only adds the studio setting, lighting, and outfit. The result looks indistinguishable from an $800+ professional studio session.

I only have regular phone photos. Will it work?

That's exactly what SnapiBaby was built for. Any phone photo taken in natural light is enough. The clearer the light, the better the result — but even basic indoor photos work beautifully.

How long does newborn photo editing take?

Traditional studios take 7–15 days. With SnapiBaby, your professionally edited newborn photos are ready in minutes — often before your baby wakes up from their nap.

Do I pay before seeing the result?

Never. You receive a free watermarked preview of your baby's photos first. You only pay if you love what you see. No risk, no commitment. 🔒

How much does newborn photography at home cost?

Traditional newborn studio sessions average $800–$1,200. SnapiBaby starts at $29 — for the same studio-quality result, from your couch, in minutes.

What newborn photography themes are available?

Can I print the newborn photos?
