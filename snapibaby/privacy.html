Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  |  Effective date: April 2026

Summary: We use the photos you send only to create your baby's portraits. We do not sell your data or your baby's images to anyone. Photos are deleted after delivery unless you request otherwise.

1. Who We Are

SnapiBaby ("we," "our," or "us") is an AI-powered newborn photography editing service. We receive phone photos of your newborn and return professional studio-quality portraits. Our contact email is hello@snapibaby.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Photos you submit: Images of your newborn sent to us via WhatsApp or our platform for editing purposes.
  • Baby's name: Optionally provided by you to personalize the experience.
  • Contact information: Your name, email address, and/or phone number, used to deliver your photos and respond to support requests.
  • Payment information: Processed securely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Usage data: Browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site (collected via cookies for analytics).

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

SnapiBaby processes photos of infants and newborns at the explicit request of their parents or legal guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13. All submissions are made by adults on behalf of their children. If you are a parent and believe we have received your child's information without your consent, contact us immediately at hello@snapibaby.com.

In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we:

  • Do not display advertising to users based on children's images.
  • Do not sell or share children's images with third parties for any commercial purpose.
  • Delete submitted photos within 30 days of delivery, unless you explicitly request otherwise.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your AI-edited newborn portraits.
  • To communicate with you about your order.
  • To process payments via Stripe.
  • To improve our service quality.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your photos to train AI models without your explicit written consent.

5. How We Share Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Stripe — for secure payment processing.
  • Cloud storage providers — temporarily, to store and deliver your photos.
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law.

6. California Residents — CCPA Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell it).
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights.

To exercise these rights, email us at hello@snapibaby.com.

7. Data Retention

Photos submitted by you are retained for up to 30 days after delivery to allow for any correction requests, then permanently deleted. Order records and contact information may be retained for up to 12 months for legal and accounting purposes.

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption, to protect your data in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

9. Your Rights (GDPR — EU Residents)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at hello@snapibaby.com to submit a request.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies to operate our website. We may use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, please contact:

SnapiBaby
Email: hello@snapibaby.com
