# Skill: copywriting
## Depends on: product-marketing-context

## O que faz
Escreve copy de alta conversao para qualquer elemento do SnapiBaby seguindo frameworks comprovados.

## Frameworks disponiveis
### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
P: "Studio newborn photography costs +"
A: "And you have to schedule weeks in advance, travel with a newborn, wait 3 weeks for results"
S: "SnapiBaby delivers studio-quality portraits from your phone in 8 minutes. From ."

### AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)
Attention: Headline com beneficio especifico e numero
Interest: Prova social + mecanismo unico
Desire: Visualizacao do resultado + urgencia
Action: CTA com beneficio embutido

### Before/After/Bridge
Before: "Expensive studios, scheduling headaches, tired newborn out of home"
After: "Professional portraits ready in 8 minutes, baby stays home"
Bridge: "SnapiBaby AI"

## Regras de voz da marca SnapiBaby
- Tom: Caloroso, maternal, confiante (nao corporativo)
- POV: "Your baby" nao "the baby"
- Numeros: sempre especificos ( nao "affordable", 8 minutes nao "fast")
- Emocao: apelar para memoria e legado, nao so estetica
- Urgencia: real (bebe cresce rapido) nao artificial

## Output
Headline alternativa + subheadline + CTA para qualquer elemento
