# Skill: cold-email
## Depends on: product-marketing-context

## O que faz
Escreve sequencias de cold email para outreach de mom blogs, influenciadores e parceiros.

## Targets para SnapiBaby
1. Mom bloggers (DA >20, audiencia de maes com filhos <1 ano)
2. Baby photographers (parceria de referral)
3. Maternidade/hospital blogs
4. Grupos de Facebook de maes (admin outreach)
5. Influenciadoras de maternidade (10k-500k seguidores)

## Template — Mom Blog Outreach
Subject: "Your readers could save + on newborn photos"
---
Hi [Name],

I love your post on [specific post] — the tip about [specific detail] was gold.

Quick question: have you heard of AI newborn photography? It's blowing up right now.

SnapiBaby transforms a simple phone photo into a studio-quality newborn portrait in 8 minutes — for  instead of the + a studio charges. Free preview before paying.

Your readers are exactly who this helps. I'd love to:
- Give you a free session to try it yourself
- Offer your readers an exclusive 20% discount code
- Write a guest post if that's something you do

Worth a quick chat?

[Name]
SnapiBaby
---

## Sequencia (3 emails)
Email 1 (dia 0): Pitch + oferta de teste gratis
Email 2 (dia 4): Follow-up com resultado de outra mae
Email 3 (dia 10): Ultimo contato + urgencia suave

## Output
Sequencia completa personalizada para o target especifico
