# Skill: ad-creative
## Depends on: product-marketing-context, paid-ads, copywriting

## O que faz
Cria briefs e copy para criativos de anuncio do SnapiBaby.

## Formatos
- Stories/Reels: 1080x1920px, 15-30s
- Feed: 1080x1080px ou 1080x1350px
- Static: imagem unica com texto overlay

## Top performing concepts para newborn AI
1. BEFORE/AFTER REVEAL: foto celular borrada -> retrato profissional (mais testado, melhor CVR)
2. PRICE ANCHOR: " studio vs  SnapiBaby" — side by side
3. TESTIMONIAL: mae real + retrato do bebe + quote emocional
4. PROCESS: screen recording do upload -> tema -> resultado em 8 min
5. SOCIAL PROOF: "Join 18,247 moms" + grid de retratos

## Copy formulas para headline de ad
- "[Number] moms ditched the  studio for this"
- "Your phone photo -> studio portrait in 8 minutes"
- "Free preview: see your baby as a Princess/Astronaut"
- "Why pay  when AI does it for "

## Output
Brief visual + headline + primary text + CTA para cada conceito
